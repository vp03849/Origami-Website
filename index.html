Interesting Facts about Chameleons

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are apart of the iguana suborder
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Fascinating Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Engrossing Facts about Camels

  • During sandstorms, camels have the ability to shut their nostrils
  • Camels can travel up to 40 miles per hour
  • A camel can drink up to 40 gallons of water at once

Riveting Facts about Bears

  1. There are 8 unique species of bears
  2. Polar bears, can weigh between 900 and 1500 pounds, and between 6 and 10 feet
  3. Grizzly bears posess a biting force that is enough to crush a bowling ball

Intriguing facts about Pandas

  • Pandas have a wrist bone, which extends from their hand. They use this, sort of like a thumb, and this wrist bone helps them grip food
  • Pandas spend up to 16 hours eating food, in the form of bamboo
  • Pandas defecate about 40 times a day

Absorbing facts about Cicadas

  1. There are about 300 species of cicada, and they vary between 2.2 to 5 centimeters long
  2. A few cicada calls can be heard up to 1.5 kilometers away
  3. The lifespan of a cicada ranges between 4 and 17 years
